Hello Foodies

Barkaas Arabic Cuisine is remarkably known for its Mandi Biryani. The moist and dewy texture of Mandi biryani is a madcap for many biryani lovers. It is a delicious combination of boiled rice, meat and fusion spices. Barkaas also serves soups, starters, grills, BBQ’S. We always take a pledge to serve you with a huge halal menu right from the core of Arab delicacy.

Chicken Faham

The recipe that makes your mouth drooling for non-veg cravings. For this recipe, chicken is usually marinated and grilled with fusion spices and served along with fresh coriander, side dip.

Crunchy Paneer

Soft and fresh paneer with authentic spices deep-fried and served with green chutney & fries.

Turkish Grill

Grilled chicken with Turkish flavours and spices. One can experience the flaky texture layer by layer that comes apart while eating.

Grilled Fish Skewers

It’s difficult to beat these aromatic fish skewers. Marinated in lemon juice, garlic, and green herbs, these succulent fish grills taste incredible served straight off the grill.

Jalapeno Cheese Crisps

Jalapeno is a hot green chilli pepper, It is based from Mexican countries, these crisps are coated with bread from outside and cheese dip from inside. Then it is served with green chutney.
